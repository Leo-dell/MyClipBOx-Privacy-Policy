MyClipBox Privacy Policy

Last updated: [December 11, 2025]

1. Introduction

MyClipBox ("we", "our app") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

MyClipBox is an application that operates completely offline and stores all data locally on your device. The information that the application manages includes:

  • Video URLs: Links to videos from YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, and Vimeo that you add to the application
  • Video Metadata: Titles, durations, and thumbnails extracted from the links
  • Custom Categories: Organization that you create for your videos
  • User Preferences: Application settings saved locally

Important: All this information is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to external servers or third parties.

3. Permission Usage

MyClipBox requires the following permissions to function correctly:

  • Internet and Network State: Only to extract metadata (titles, durations, thumbnails) from video URLs that you provide. This information is temporarily downloaded and stored locally on your device.

We do not access your contacts, location, camera, microphone, or other sensitive information.

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your Android device using SharedPreferences and cache files. The data includes:

  • Saved video list
  • Categories and custom organization
  • Cached thumbnails for offline viewing
  • Application settings

We do not use cloud services. All your data remains on your device and can be completely removed by uninstalling the application.

5. Third-Party Interactions

MyClipBox may open video links in the official YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram applications installed on your device. This functionality only works if you have these applications installed and is designed to provide you with a better user experience.

When you open a video in these external applications, their own privacy policies apply. We do not share information with these platforms; we only facilitate opening links.

6. Security

Although MyClipBox stores data locally, we recommend:

  • Not sharing your device with unauthorized persons
  • Using your device's security features (screen lock, etc.)
  • Uninstalling the application if you sell or give away your device

7. Your Rights

Since all data is stored on your device, you have complete control over it:

  • You can delete any video or category at any time from the application
  • You can uninstall the application to remove all data
  • Data is not synced or automatically backed up to the cloud

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. We will notify you of any significant changes through an application update. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects when the last modification was made.

9. Biometric Privacy

If MyClipBox offers biometric privacy features (such as fingerprint lock or facial recognition), this biometric data is managed solely by the Android operating system and is never collected or stored by our application.
